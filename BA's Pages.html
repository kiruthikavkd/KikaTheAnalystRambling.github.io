How to start in a new team (for those fast-paced days)

  • 15 mins read

Starting in a new team can be overwhelming especially if you haven't done it before. This read will guide you on some first steps and how to stay on top of things when it seems that you are in college learning new stuff every single day. The first few months are agreeably difficult but with a few steps it could be easier if not stress-free.

Wondering why should you read through my ramblings?

You don't need to. But if you have got 30 mins there are some pretty awesome tips on how you could tackle all those difficult moments as a business analyst.

Why hear my tips?
I have been in the same situation especially with 7 complex products implemented in 8 years. You can imagine me getting comfortable with a team and then immediately pulled into another new one. After 2 such instances I knew this was going to be my new normal and hence made attempts to make my life easier. As a business analyst's usual, I asked what do I need to solve this problem of being with a new team every year and the answer were these tips. I shared these with some of my colleagues and it definitely made their transitions over teams smoother. Hopefully it will make your transition a bit easier too.
